Hack Volusia

Saturday December 2nd, 2017 @ The Daytona Beach UCF Business Incubator.

About

Hack Volusia aims to bring STEM enthusiasts from local Colleges & Universities together in a business environment to help grow Volusia County's Economy.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is best described as an “invention marathon”. Anyone who has an interest in technology attends a hackathon to learn, build & share their creations. We will have plenty of workshops to help new hackers along with mentors from various local companies.

Cost

Hack Volusia is completely free thanks to our generous sponsors. We will have plenty of food for our competitors including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks (please fill out dietary restrictions when registering so we may accommodate you). Parking is free but limited so please carpool.

Rules & Additional Information

Teams of up to 1-4 hackers may compete there will be team building before and after the opening ceremony. We ask that projects are not worked on before the Hackathon begins to keep an equal playing field. All participants must agree to the MLH Code of conduct located HERE.

When & Where

Saturday December 2nd 9AM-9PM, at the UCF Business Incubator in Daytona Beach, FL 601 Innovation Way (right next to the airport).
